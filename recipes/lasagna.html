Lasagna

Description

Garfield meal

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  4. Brown ground beef or sausage in a pan, then add marinara sauce and simmer.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and seasonings like salt, pepper, and basil.
  6. Layer a baking dish with sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  7. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  8. Cover with foil, bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes until bubbly.
