Shaorma

Description

This chicken shawarma is made with flavorful chicken thighs marinated in a spiced yogurt. Slice the cooked chicken and serve in pita bread with tomato, sliced onion, and my homemade tahini sauce for a popular Middle Eastern sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Garlic cloves
  • Yogurt
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Ground cumin
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with garlic, yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, and paprika for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat your grill or pan to medium-high heat.
  3. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers (if grilling) or place them in the pan.
  4. Cook the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side until fully cooked and golden brown.
  5. Once cooked, remove the chicken from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Slice the chicken into thin strips.
  7. Serve the shawarma in pita or flatbread with your choice of toppings like tomatoes, cucumbers, and tahini sauce.
